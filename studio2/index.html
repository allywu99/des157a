9.8.2020

During the height of quarantine, I wanted to ignite my former passions and get back into exploring photography, specifically, light photography. I had always been fascinated by striking light paintings captured by phtographers like Brandon Woelfel and Jessica Kobeissi. To mimic the utopian color scheme and ethereal style of their photos, I dragged my two housemates along, grabbed some fairy lights, and went to work with my Canon 70D.
